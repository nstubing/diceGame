Welcome to PAY UP!

The most fun dice gambling game on the internet!

How To Play: Player one rolls his two four-sided dice and he establishes his numbers. After that its all up to player two, he must roll a four-sided, six-sided, eight-sided, ten-sided, 12-sided, and a twenty-sided die hoping to roll one of the same numbers that player one previously established. The initial bet is $1 per player into the pot, if player two rolls one of player one's numbers then he wins. If player two doesn't roll one of player one's numbers on the first roll then he moves on to the next die, decreasing his odds of winning and going through two more betting cycles at most.
